**Style notes:**

- Many English terms are preserved in Bulgarian contexts (SVM, PCA, CNN, etc.)
- Transliterations like "фийчър" and "овърфитване" are standard
- Mathematical notation (X, y, W, b) always uses Latin script
- Code-switching between English and Bulgarian is common in practice
