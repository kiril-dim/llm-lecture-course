From Large Language Models to AI Agents

An applied view on modern NLP exploring the most recent advances

Course Information

Format: 3 Academic hours: 2 lecture + 1 exercise

Prerequisites: 1st and 2nd year mathematics and computer science courses, basic probability, linear algebra, basic Python

Examination: 60% final project + 40% written test
